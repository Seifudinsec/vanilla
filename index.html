01 — The Bean

Sourced from the world's finest growers

We travel directly to small-batch farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Sumatra. Every bean is hand-selected, graded, and cupped before it earns a place in our roastery. We don't just buy coffee — we build relationships with the people who grow it.

01
02 — The Roast

Precision roasted in small batches

Our custom drum roaster transforms green beans into liquid gold. Each batch is profiled to the bean's unique character — bringing out chocolate notes in the Brazilian, floral tones in the Ethiopian, and a bold body in the Sumatran. Small batches mean every cup is fresh.

02
03 — The Pour

Every cup is a craft

Our baristas are trained in the art of precision brewing. From the perfect espresso extraction to the delicate pour-over, every drink is made with intention. We don't rush — because great coffee takes time. Taste the difference that care makes.
